Questions We Get a Lot

Is this legally registered?

We're a student initiative under our college society, currently in the process of registering as a non-profit. Everything is documented and transparent.

How do I know the cause is real?

Every cause goes through a physical visit by one of our volunteers. We attach proof of visit and document checks before listing anyone.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes. When submitting your screenshot, you can choose to stay anonymous. Your name won't appear anywhere without your permission.

How do you fund Sahaay itself?

We're entirely self-funded by the volunteers. Domain, hosting — all from our own pockets because we believe in what we're building.

Can I suggest a family who needs help?

Yes! Reach out via the contact page or DM on Instagram. We'll send a volunteer to verify within 3–5 days if it's in our area.

What if the goal is already met?

Since you pay directly there's no refund needed. We close the listing once the goal is reached and post a completion update.
