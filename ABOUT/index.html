Know About Us

Sahaay empowers underprivileged children through education, food, and care for a brighter future.

Sahaay was founded on 15th August 2022 with a mission to support underprivileged children. We provide education, meals, and a safe environment where every child can grow with dignity.

Our vision is to create equal opportunities for every child to learn, dream, and succeed.
